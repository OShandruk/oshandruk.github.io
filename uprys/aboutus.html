our values

Cultivate Community First

UpRys is designed to help facilitate, as seamless as we can, process improvement for the causes that matter most. This pertains to key elements of their operations including stakeholder engagement (internal and external), adminstation, IT, human resources, and financial departments. This is how we drive revenue.

Passionate About Causes

Our primary goal is to help organizations make an impact in their community by elevating their admin operations and workforce. Everything we do must begin and end with a drive to continually improve their operations and enhance interaction with their communities.

Be One Team

We will be at our best when we work together. Clarity in thought and honest communication will be the defining attribute for everyone we work with. This applies internally and externally.

Time is Not Money

We will be driven first by purpose and the cause’s needs. This will allow us to iterate and craft the most unique experience for them. Only then can we focus on profits.

Our Services

UpRys combination of experienced professionals and diverse pool of freelance partners focus on six key areas of service:

  • Finance and Accounting
  • Operations
  • Workforce Management
  • Marketing and Communications
  • Information Technology
  • Leadership

These focus areas, chosen for their necessity and impact in today’s market, help to provide a well-rounded view of the overall business model to guide organizations toward sustainable success.

Whether it is one department, or the full business model in need of streamlining – UpRys provides the strategy and delivers on the solution, so leaders can focus on mission-critical work, resulting in a more cost-effective, efficient organization. To further deliver on our mission, UpRys gives back a percentage of profits to our nonprofit partners in the community.
